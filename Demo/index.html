Building a Reliable, Secure, and Scalable Digital Foundation for Industry

Headquartered in Singapore for over a decade, SENWAIY TECH is a specialized system integrator for FMCS, SCADA, and industrial automation. Combining regional responsiveness with the engineering precision of our Taiwan tech centers, we deliver high-standard solutions across High-Tech Electronics, Heavy Industry, and Public Infrastructure. Unlike typical distributors, our engineering-first approach ensures scalable and resilient results for industrial clients throughout the ASEAN region.

As an authorized AVEVA distributor in Singapore, SENWAIY TECH builds secure digital foundations for modern operations. Through strategic alliances with partners like Penguin Solution, Fortinet, and Rockwell Automation, we integrate cybersecurity, infrastructure, and data platforms into a robust industrial ecosystem. We empower businesses in Singapore and Malaysia to maintain a competitive edge through seamless, data-driven transformation.

Integration Capabilities Spanning the Complete Technology Stack

From ground-level devices to upper-layer applications, Senwaiy delivers a comprehensive and scalable industrial software integration architecture. Understanding the complexity and diversity of industrial environments, we are committed to creating flexible and robust integration solutions that adapt to the needs of different industries and enterprises of all sizes.

Through standardized communication protocols and open architecture design, we help enterprises break down information silos and achieve seamless cross-system, cross-platform data flow. This comprehensive architecture enables enterprises to seamlessly connect all operational segments — from real-time data acquisition at the device layer, to production scheduling at the management layer, to decision analytics at the enterprise layer — achieving end-to-end digitalization and intelligent transformation from device to decision.

Device Layer

PLC controllers, sensors, instrumentation, robotic arms, automation equipment.

Control Layer

SCADA supervisory systems, HMI interfaces, batch control, real-time control systems.

Management Layer

MES manufacturing execution systems, quality management, equipment management, production scheduling.

Enterprise Layer

ERP integration, database systems, business intelligence (BI), reporting and analytics, alarm management.

Explore Senwaiy Products

AUG｜Centralize Industrial Communication in One Platform

AUG is a powerful industrial communication integration platform designed to connect systems, equipment, and PLCs using commonly adopted automation protocols.

By eliminating the need for multiple drivers, AUG simplifies protocol conversion and enables real-time data to be seamlessly consolidated into centralized databases.

Discover AUG

CGW｜Build a Reliable Foundation for IIoT

CGW is an industrial smart communication gateway that supports more than 15 commonly used industrial protocols and enables connectivity via OPC UA and cloud platforms, forming a solid foundation for Industrial IoT (IIoT).

Operating as a standalone platform, CGW reduces the need for multiple drivers on PCs, simplifies system architecture, and reliably aggregates and standardizes data from PLCs and field devices for seamless integration with SCADA and MES systems.

Discover CGW

Inspec｜Paperless Inspections Made Simple

Inspec is a smart inspection system that enables paperless and digitalized inspection workflows, supporting task assignment, accurate reporting, and automated report generation.

By consolidating inspection data entered by field personnel, Inspec organizes and analyzes records into structured reports, allowing teams to track progress in real time via mobile devices and improve inspection efficiency and quality.

Discover Inspec

MGS｜Ensure Critical Alerts Are Never Missed

MGS is an automated alarm and event notification solution that supports more than 10 delivery channels, enabling seamless integration of events and alarms across enterprise and plant environments.

By classifying and grouping alarms based on predefined rules, MGS captures events from multiple sources and ensures the right notifications are delivered to the right people, preventing critical alerts from being missed.

Discover MGS

Modbus Gateway｜Standardize Modbus Data Across Systems

A smart gateway specializing in Modbus communication, the Modbus Gateway enables seamless conversion between Serial and Ethernet interfaces.

Struggling with multiple Modbus protocols and device connectivity? The Modbus Gateway simplifies operations and unlocks the full potential of your industrial connectivity.

Discover Modbus Gateway

RGS｜Turn Operational Data into Management Insights

RGS is a centralized report generation system that consolidates operational data from multiple sources into standardized and visualized reports.

By automating report creation and scheduling, RGS helps teams monitor performance, analyze trends, and support faster, more informed decision-making.

Discover RGS
