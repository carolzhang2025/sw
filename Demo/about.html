About Senwaiy Tech

Premier FMCS & Industrial Automation Solutions in ASEAN.

Headquartered in Singapore for over a decade, SENWAIY TECH is a specialized system integrator for industrial automation, SCADA, and Facility Management Control Systems (FMCS).

We operate in parallel with our technical centers in Taiwan, combining regional responsiveness with engineering precision.

Unlike typical distributors, we are an engineering-first company with a track record across diverse mission-critical sectors. Our expertise successfully transfers high-standard engineering practices between High-Tech Electronics, Heavy Industry, FMCG, and Public Infrastructure.

Whether optimizing a continuous process plant or commissioning a complex facility management system, SENWAIY TECH delivers scalable, resilient solutions tailored to industrial clients across Singapore, Malaysia, and the wider ASEAN region.

Why Top-Tier Enterprises Trust Us

At SENWAIY TECH, we build critical infrastructure for industry leaders. Our track record spans across high-tech electronics, public infrastructure, and process manufacturing.

Core Engineering Capabilities

Industrial Automation & Control

We implement fault-tolerant control architectures that stabilize production. From standalone PLC programming to plant-wide SCADA networks, our systems reduce human error. We integrate major brands (AVEVA, Siemens, Schneider, Rockwell, Mitsubishi) to create automated environments tailored to your manufacturing logic.

Advanced FMCS

For semiconductor and high-tech manufacturing, utility stability dictates production yield. Our FMCS (Facility Management Control System)centralizes control of HVAC, UPW, Gas, and Chemicals into a single command center. We provide 24/7 monitoring with millisecond-level precision to prevent downtime.

Power Monitoring & Energy Management

Rising energy costs and strict ESG mandates require precise visibility. We deploy Power Monitoring Systems (PMS) that go beyond simple metering. Our systems analyze power quality, detect harmonics, and provide granular data for ISO and IEC compliance, supporting your carbon footprint goals.

OT/IT System Integration

Data silos block Industry 4.0 progress. SENWAIY TECH bridges Operational Technology (OT) and Information Technology (IT). We use industrial protocols (Modbus, OPC UA, MQTT) to enable seamless data flow from the factory floor to your MES, ERP, or Cloud platforms.
