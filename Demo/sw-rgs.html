Report Generator System

Unify your enterprise data and gain full visibility into production with intuitive, real-time visual analytics.

What is RGS ?

RGS (Report Generator System) is an automated platform specifically designed for IT and OT professionals in the manufacturing industry. It is engineered to aggregate data from across the entire factory and enterprise. By integrating heterogeneous data from various systems, RGS helps businesses establish a centralized data hub, transforming fragmented information into standardized reports and laying a solid foundation for smart manufacturing.

The system effectively addresses the common pitfalls of traditional Excel-based reporting, such as tedious manual entry, performance lags with large datasets, and fragmented management. Featuring a rich library of visual templates, RGS automatically generates and distributes reports based on predefined schedules. This allows managers to track critical production trends—from shop floor anomalies to ERP metrics—turning complex data into precise, actionable insights.

Features

Data Visualization

RGS transforms data into intuitive visual charts, enabling managers to identify trends, assess risks, and enhance decision-making efficiency.

Full Process Automation

Automate repetitive manual tasks with scheduled report generation and distribution, significantly optimizing corporate operational efficiency.

Multi-Source Integration

Consolidate historical and real-time data from diverse sources into a single report for seamless cross-system analysis.

Smart Dashboards & War Room

Utilize built-in modules to turn static data into dynamic interactive dashboards, easily establishing a corporate smart command center.

How RGS works

RGS automates complex data processing. In just four simple steps, factory-wide data is transformed into high-value visual insights, reducing manual costs and enhancing management transparency.

01

Data Integration

Connect SQL, ERP, and real-time databases to ensure seamless synchronization across heterogeneous systems.

02

Template Design

Utilize built-in templates to intuitively design custom interactive reports and smart dashboards.

03

Automated Execution

Set custom schedules (daily/weekly) for the system to automatically retrieve data and generate reports.

04

Smart Delivery

With one-click subscription and automated alerts, stakeholders can access the latest insights anytime online.

Benefits

Maximize Productivity

Eliminate manual entry and Excel lags to save significant time for IT/OT staff, allowing them to focus on core data analysis.

Centralized Data Visibility

Consolidate fragmented reports from production to ERP, breaking down information silos for unified data management.

Real-time Decision Making

Empower managers to pinpoint anomalies and shorten response times with real-time dashboards, optimizing production scheduling.

Accelerate Digital Transformation

Upgrade from traditional Excel to an automated visual system, seamlessly bridging the gap to Industry 4.0 and data-driven operations.

Customer story

Smart Water Resource Management

Amidst extreme weather, precise water allocation is a core challenge for smart cities. Leveraging digital technology to monitor storage and integrate regional resources is essential for enhancing management resilience and ensuring a stable water supply.

The client is a regional waterworks authority managing a vast supply network across 15 reservoirs. Currently, the client faces challenges of fragmented data and a lack of real-time information. There is an urgent need for an automated platform to transform complex storage and consumption data into intuitive "remaining supply days" and visual dashboards, enabling managers to make precise allocation decisions at critical moments.

Managing 15 reservoirs across different regions involves massive datasets, making it difficult for traditional management models to support real-time responses and accurate forecasting.

  • Fragmented Data Silos

    Data from various reservoirs were scattered, making it impossible to gain a comprehensive view of the regional water supply status.

  • Inefficient Manual Calculation

    Converting water volume into "days of supply" relied on manual tasks, delaying critical drought early-warning information.

  • Lack of Real-time Decision Support

    Without mobile access, management struggled to monitor key water conditions and make timely allocation decisions.

By implementing the RGS (Report Generator System), the client transformed scattered, static data into a dynamic information flow with high strategic value.

  • Regional Visual Dashboard

    Real-time visualization of 15 reservoirs, displaying storage percentages, full-level alerts, and days of remaining supply.

  • Web-based Monitoring System

    Stakeholders can access real-time information of the regional water system via a web browser anytime, anywhere.

  • Automated Early-Warning Reports

    Automatically generated comparison reports provide a critical basis for drought warnings, pressure reduction, and water rationing plans.

  • Mobile Insight Integration

    Real-time mobile access enables more efficient execution of reservoir discharge processes during full-level scenarios.

RGS successfully established a data-driven decision-making mechanism, elevating the client from individual site management to intelligent regional dispatching.

  • Instant Mobile Access

    Managers obtain first-hand water status via mobile devices, significantly shortening response times.

  • Digital Analysis & Continuous Improvement

    Centralized online management and report subscription facilitate thorough data reviews and optimize water utilization.

  • Minimized Response Time

    Flexible cross-regional water allocation is now achievable, drastically improving crisis management and dispatching efficiency.
