Message Gateway System

Zero-Lag Factory Management: Automated Event Integration & Instant Notification.

What is MGS ?

An Automated Real-time Notification Platform Built to End Alarm Fatigue MGS is an automated hub that integrates plant-wide events and enterprise alerts. In complex environments with scattered subsystems, it tackles the "boiling frog" crisis where critical warnings are often buried under information overload. Designed to eliminate notification delays, MGS ensures every vital anomaly is instantly recognized, removing the fear of missing crucial alerts during high-stakes operations.

A Smart Cross-System Hub: Delivering the Right Message to the Right Person Moving beyond basic pagers, MGS is a smart hub supporting 7 channels, including LINE, Teams, SMS, and Voice. It unifies data across systems, transforming fragmented signals into actionable insights. By ensuring that the right message reaches the designated personnel, MGS resolves response inefficiencies at their source, significantly strengthening corporate operational resilience.

Features

Multi-channel Notification Dispatch

Supports diverse channels like LINE, WeChat, Email, Voice, Teams, and Displays, ensuring every critical alert is delivered.

Flexible Site-wide Application

Bridges enterprise communications and shop-floor equipment anomalies into a unified, automated management platform.

Heterogeneous System Integration

Seamlessly connects SCADA and ERP systems while supporting protocols like SQL, CSV, XML, and OPC DA/UA.

Real-time Monitoring & Proactive Alerting

Provides 24/7 monitoring across all endpoints; sends instant alerts upon anomaly detection to minimize response time.

Intelligent Hierarchical Escalation

Dispatches alerts based on severity and automatically escalates to management if an issue remains unresolved.

Secure Group & Permission Management

Features role-based access control and customizable recipient groups to ensure precise information delivery to the right person.

How MGS works

MGS (Message Gateway System) leverages a robust technical architecture to seamlessly bridge scattered site signals and enterprise data. Through four key stages—Acquisition, Filtering, Dispatch, and Tracking—it establishes a fully automated monitoring loop, transforming chaotic information flows into precise actionable insights to maximize corporate response efficiency.

01

Data Acquisition & Integration

Collects real-time event signals from factory equipment, PLC/SCADA systems, or enterprise ERPs using protocols like OPC DA/UA, SQL databases, or APIs.

02

Intelligent Logic & Filtering

The system cross-references data with preset conditions, filtering out noise and prioritizing alerts based on severity and shift schedules.

03

Instant Multi-channel Dispatch

Transforms processed data into standardized messages and pushes them instantly to the designated personnel via 7 channels, including LINE, Teams, Voice, and SMS.

04

Automated Escalation & Tracking

If an alert remains unacknowledged, the system triggers an escalation to management. All dispatch and feedback logs are recorded for future analysis and optimization.

Benefits

Maximize Response Speed

Instant alerts shorten MTTR and effectively reduce production losses caused by downtime.

Eliminate Alarm Fatigue

Smart filtering reduces noise and interruptions, focusing personnel on core tasks.

Transparent Accountability

Centralized logs streamline event tracking and ensure precise management accountability.

Zero-Miss & High Resilience

Multi-channel escalation ensures zero missed alerts, safeguarding corporate operational safety.

Customer story

Precision Mfg Resilience: MGS Unified Automated Notifications

The rise of Smart Cockpits makes automotive displays a cabin core. Global demand for quality and stability drives manufacturers toward smart management, utilizing digital tools to enhance resilience and meet ESG goals for a competitive edge.

The client is a leading international manufacturer of automotive display glass, serving as an OEM for top-tier brands like Continental and BMW. Their facility operates highly complex automated lines, including motherboard cutting, CNC edge grinding, AR (Sputtering) washing/screen printing, and ASAG (Anti-fingerprint spraying). The operation relies heavily on real-time monitoring and high-precision equipment maintenance.

In high-precision manufacturing, any monitoring oversight can jeopardize production uptime and strict quality standards. The client faced several critical challenges during their digital transition :

  • Delayed Emergency Response

    The lack of instant notifications hindered rapid reaction to equipment anomalies, leading to costly unplanned downtime.

  • Manual Maintenance Risks

    Relying on manual tracking for vast equipment arrays led to missed maintenance schedules and frequent component failures.

  • Lack of Data Visibility

    Isolated energy consumption data across departments created information silos, hindering energy-saving and ESG targets.

Implementing MGS as the central hub to integrate equipment signals and systems, creating an automated and transparent notification network :

  • Instant Anomaly Alerts

    Pushes real-time alerts (CNC, AR, ASAG) via LINE, ensuring immediate response to equipment anomalies.

  • Preventive Maintenance

    Automated reminders for routine maintenance shift operations from reactive repairs to proactive prevention.

  • Transparent Utility Reporting

    Broadcasts water and electricity cost reports, ensuring visibility to drive departmental energy-saving goals.

  • Efficient ERP Broadcasting

    Syncs with ERP for rapid delivery of administrative news, optimizing corporate internal communication.

Through MGS digital management, the client enhanced operational resilience and global competitiveness, achieving dual optimization of efficiency and ESG goals :

  • 50% Faster Response

    Hierarchical notifications eliminate communication delays, doubling the speed of anomaly handling.

  • Reliable Production Uptime

    Significantly reduced missed maintenance incidents, effectively preventing production halts due to equipment failure.

  • 80% ESG Achievement

    Transparent energy data drove collective saving efforts, boosting target achievement rates from 50% to 80%.

  • Global Competitive Edge

    Smart management won deep trust from brands like BMW and Continental, securing multiple high-value international orders.
